How this feeds the rubric

  • Preparation = pattern of P's vs. X's in the weekly cells. Consistent P's anchor high ratings; repeated X's lower them.
  • Participation = starred cells (standout contributions) plus absence of 0's (attendance).
  • Professionalism = flag column patterns plus notes about device policy, punctuality, respect.
  • Growth = trajectory across the semester. Early X's followed by P's = growth. Consistent X's = flat.

Intervention thresholds

  • Two flags in a row → brief conversation with the student
  • Three flags total → formal check-in meeting
  • Any "!!!" → same-day outreach
  • Pattern of X's on a whole team → team-level conversation
