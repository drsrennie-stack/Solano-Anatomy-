BIO 004 · Clinical Professionalism
Instructor Rubric
Summer 2026 · Dr. Rennie
Rate the student on each of four dimensions using the 4-point scale. Base scores on observed behavior across the preceding four weeks: homework bundle completion, application exercise participation, punctuality, class engagement, team contribution, and growth trajectory. This score contributes 2/3 of the student's Clinical Professionalism grade. Peer evaluation contributes the remaining 1/3. When complete, click Save as PDF, then upload the saved PDF to Canvas.
Four-Dimension Rubric
Dimension What it measures Rating
Preparation
Arrives on time and ready. Has completed homework bundles and pre-work. Shows familiarity with the week's case. Brings required materials. Does not need to be caught up by teammates.
Participation
Contributes substantively to team discussion. Voices anatomical reasoning. Engages with case material actively. Participates in Wednesday application exercises. Asks questions. Not passive or silent.
Professionalism
Respectful communication with teammates and instructor. Reliable with commitments. Owns mistakes. Follows device and lab policies. Behaves like a future clinician. Maintains integrity in team documentation.
Growth
Accepts feedback constructively. Improves from week to week. Supports teammates' learning. Moves from dependence to independence. Shows trajectory upward rather than flat or declining.
